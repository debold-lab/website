Myosin V Collaboration receives Editors' Pick from JBC

By The Debold Lab | October 16, 2020

In collaboration with the Yengo Lab (Penn State) and Thomas Lab (University of Minnesota) our paper “FRET and optical trapping reveal mechanisms of actin-activation of the power stroke and phosphate-release in myosin V” was accepted to the Journal of Biological Chemistry and was chosen to featured be as an Editors’ Pick for “providing an exceptional contribution to the field”.
